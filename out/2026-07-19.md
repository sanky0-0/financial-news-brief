**Why this matters:**
- South Africa’s nuclear recommitment despite recent safety incidents at Eskom’s Koeberg plant signals strong political will for baseload power, boosting long-term uranium demand — watch for upside in producers like Cameco (CCJ) and Kazatomprom (KAP), with the uranium spot price already near $100/lb.
